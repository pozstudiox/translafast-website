Frequently Asked Questions

What is TranslaFast?

TranslaFast is a browser extension that instantly translates web pages while respecting privacy and displaying no ads.

Which languages are supported?

We support over 178 languages, including Turkish, English, German, Japanese, Russian, and many more.

Is TranslaFast free?

Yes. TranslaFast is completely free and never asks for your personal information.

Is my data safe?

Yes. All translation processing happens on your device. No data is sent to any server.

Does TranslaFast show ads?

No. There are no ads or analytics in our app. It offers a clean, fast, and ad-free experience.

Does it work offline?

No. An internet connection is required for translation engines to function properly.

How accurate are the translations?

Users can switch between engines like Google, LibreTranslate, and DeepL. Choose the one that provides the best result for your needs.

Which browsers are supported?

Currently, TranslaFast is fully compatible with Google Chrome. Support for other browsers is under development.

Does translation affect the website's design?

No. TranslaFast only changes text content and keeps the layout and design intact.

Can I set a default language?

Yes. You can set your preferred default language and translation engine from the settings menu.

Is TranslaFast open source?

Yes. You can view and contribute to our open-source project on GitHub.

Is there a way to get support?

Yes. You can send your questions and feedback to pozstudiosh@gmail.com.
